the challenge
The challenge of this project was to create a clear, responsive website for VidiVaka WebSite. VidiVaka is a medium intended to inform the digital generation, which the news usually gets them online. Vidivaka processes, develops, and produces news that we share on social networks.
my role
This project is one of my projects as a student at UX/UI Design Academy at Brainster. This was a team project and I was included in the whole UX/UI Design process

competitor analysis
The first step of the process I took was Competitor Analysis. We searched the market and found few websites as a direct competitors. We chose two of them: Vox and NowThis. After researching them we came up with some strengths and weaknesses.
strenghts
- Load more button - eliminates the endless scroll
- Clear card design
- Hero section (organization and readability)
weaknesses
- Hamburger menu on desktop
- Social media in he–∞der
- The podcast is not visible on the home page

personas
After analysing competitors and researching user reviews. We gained a more clear picture of the type of persons who might be interested in websites such as VidiVaka. This users are mostly people between the ages of 20 and 40. They usually get the news online. Guided by that we made the personas.
user flow
We wanted to take a clear picture of the users navigating through the site while interacting with the new features. We created the user flow for searching posts and for requesting stories.
wireframes
We created responsive wireframes for VidiVaka to organize content and features to be more intuitive for the users. We tried to make the first fold welcoming for everyday users and the users that are first time on this website.
